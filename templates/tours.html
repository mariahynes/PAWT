St. Stephen's Green

Months Time Day
June, July, August, September 1pm Sunday
The St. Stephen's Green tour consists of 13 sculptures all located within the park. One of these works is not accessible by wheelchair.

Meet at Fusiliers Arch, just outside the main entrance to Stephen's Green and the first point on the tour.

Merrion Square Park

Dates Time Day
June 18th 1pm Saturday
July 30th 1pm Saturday
August 13th 1pm Saturday
September 10th 1pm Saturday
There are 11 sculptures in Merrion Square Park as well as the Rutland Memorial and a collection of old Dublin lampposts.

Meet at the entrance across from Leinster House (between the National Gallery and the Natural History Museum) in front of the glass pyramid.
