Walking Tours

Public Art Walking Tours is Dublin City's newest walking tour experience. On our two city centre tours, we showcase the variety and skills of Irish and International sculptors through their work in St. Stephens Green and Merrion Square Park.

We offer one-hour tours of St. Stephen's Green on Sunday afternoons, at 1pm from June to the end of September (booking essential).
